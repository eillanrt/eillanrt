8-bit lights Pattern

About

I designed my custom lights pattern with 8 LEDs. Once a desired pattern was chosen, I mapped the light patterns into binary. Where each bit represents an ON or OFF active high state of its corresponding LED. The computer runs a program written in 8086 Assembly that sends signals on every delay time to the output port via the DB25, which then the signals are simultaneously transmitted to the PCB with LEDs.
